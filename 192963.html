Welcome to flowers by Floor.

On this page I will explain a bit about myself and my passion for flowers.
I hope you will be as excited I am about flowers
​My name is Floor Verhoeven. I am 22 years old and I live in a student house with 3 other girls in Breda. I was born in Son & Breugel, Netherlands, but after my 18th birthday, we moved to Eindhoven the "big" city where I developed my passion for flowers. After I graduated from my my previous study "SintLucas" I wanted to do something else and started with the study Creative Business at Breda University of Applied Sciences. And I want to combine my little flowershop with some creative business insides.
